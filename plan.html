Career Exploration

Why a Structured Career Search Matters

A Structured Career Search = A Systematic Approach

01

Provides Clarity and Focus

Self-awareness + Market insight + Consistent action + Reflection = a clear direction instead of scattered, random applications.

02

Embraces Iteration, Not Permanence

Paths evolve as you cycle through Explore → Target → Apply → Interview → Reflect → Adjust.

03

Builds Confidence and Momentum

A structured approach breaks the process into manageable steps, making it less overwhelming and more achievable.

Timeline & Planning

Academic Year Snapshot (Reference)

  • Fall: Exploration + networking + early tech internship apps.
  • Winter: Main internship application wave + interview practice.
  • Spring: Late-cycle applications + portfolio refinement + negotiation prep.
  • Summer: Internship execution + reflection + gap filling.

Building a Weekly Routine

  • 2 hours: Exploration
  • 3 hours: Applications
  • 2 hours: Networking
  • 2 hours: Portfolio work
  • 1 hour: Reflection

Adjust based on your schedule and priorities.

Stay Organized

  • Use a spreadsheet to track applications
  • Set weekly goals and review progress
  • Schedule regular check-ins with career advisors
  • Celebrate small wins along the way

International Student Considerations

Work Authorization & Timing

  • Understand CPT/OPT timing and requirements
  • Visit the International Center for official guidance
  • Plan applications around authorization timelines

Finding Sponsorship-Friendly Employers

  • Use alumni data to identify supportive companies
  • Search job postings mentioning visa sponsorship
  • Review H1B historical databases
  • Network with international alumni in your field

Leverage Your Unique Strengths

  • Highlight cross-cultural insights and adaptability
  • Emphasize multilingual skills
  • Showcase global perspective and international experience
  • Connect with companies with global operations
